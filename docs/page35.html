Finned Fish

he left figure is X-Wing(2D-Fish). From the Locked relationship between BaseSet and CoverSet, circled digits X can be excluded from candidate digits. Large circle represent block. Even if the digit X with square is additionally present as shown on the right, the circled digit X can be excluded from the candidate digit. And the column c1 squared digit X is also excluded. Square boxed digit X is called Fish's Fin. The cell arrangement on the right is Finned Fish. In Finned Fish, since the complementary relationship is not established, there are cases where Finned Fish of 5th order or more is necessary.


This is Finned Fish. From the left is Finned X-Wing, Finned Sword Fish, Finned Jerry Fish. Yellow cell is Fish part, green cell is Fin.
